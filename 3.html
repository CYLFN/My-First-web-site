Specification

Tube bundle shall be designed for the specified differential pressure 46 bar between shell and tube side. Vendor shall: - provide adequate calculation and hydrotest procedures (in particular to guarantee the tube bundle stability during hydrotest) - clearly indicate what above in his drawings - provide a special name plate adjacent the nameplate in which is pointed out that internal components are designed for differential pressure and outlining test pressure regulations
