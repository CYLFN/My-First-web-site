TEMA

The current edition of the TEMA Standard is the Tenth Edition, published in 2019.[4] Worldwide, the TEMA Standard is used as the construction standard for most shell and tube heat exchangers.[5][6][7] The standard is composed of ten sections:[8] Nomenclature (see below) Fabrication Tolerances General Fabrication and Performance Information Installation, Operation, and Maintenance Mechanical Standards Flow Induced Vibration Thermal Relations Physical Properties of Fluids General Information Recommended Good Practice
